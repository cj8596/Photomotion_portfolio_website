About Us

With over a decade of experience in the art of visual storytelling, we are a full-service creative media production house specializing in high-impact product photography and videography. Our mission is simple yet powerful: to craft imagery that doesn't just look beautiful—but sells, inspires, and elevates brands to their fullest potential.


Rooted in both artistic vision and technical expertise, our studio is where creativity meets precision. We collaborate with brands across the fashion, beauty, lifestyle, and product industries to create content that speaks with clarity and intention. From the initial concept to the final frame, we ensure every detail aligns with your brand’s voice, goals, and audience.


Our portfolio includes a wide range of styles—from crisp, minimalist flatlays that highlight the elegance of design, to immersive product videos that captivate and convert. Whether you’re launching a new line or refreshing your brand identity, we provide visual assets that are strategic, modern, and impactful.


At the heart of our work is a deep passion for lighting, styling, and storytelling. We understand that great visuals do more than showcase a product—they tell a story, evoke emotion, and build connection. This understanding has made us a trusted creative partner for both emerging labels and established brands alike.


We don’t just create content—we create visual experiences that move people and drive results. Let us help you bring your products to life with clarity, style, and purpose.

General

How and when to pay?

Once your products are delivered to Squareshot and we confirm that the items match the proposal, we’ll send you an invoice. Once you’ve made the payment, we’ll proceed to the shooting stage.
We use Stripe, so you can pay with all major Credit/Debit cards as well as ACH transfers.

How to deliver products?

After the proposal is accepted, deliver the items to the assigned location via a drop‑off or using a major shipping provider.
Drop‑off: Mon–Fri: 9:00 AM–5:00 PM.
Do not ship or drop off your products before the proposal is accepted, and ensure it follows the Product Requirements and Delivery Checklist.

How memberships work?

Membership plans offer lower price per image, reduced turnaround, and no minimum order. Membership pricing applies to all orders placed after activation.

Is retouching included?

Yes, standard retouching is included. Complex edits like reshaping or fixing damage are charged extra.

When will images be ready?

We start shooting the next business day after payment. Delivery options:
• Standard: 6‑8 business days
• Rush: 3‑4 business days
(For orders up to 50 standard images, excluding holidays)

How to approve images?

Once you receive the approval link, you have 10 calendar days to request edits. Revisions after this period are still accepted but paid. Clearly highlight and explain your requests.

Do you provide refunds?

We do not issue refunds for completed and delivered photography, retouching, or additional services.
