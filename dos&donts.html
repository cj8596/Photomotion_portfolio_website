Do and Don’ts

DOs

  • Ship clean, damage-free products — spotless, crease-free, and in perfect condition.
  • Label everything clearly — name, SKU, or product code to avoid mix-ups.
  • Include a packing list — with item names and quantities.
  • Package safely — bubble wrap, paper fill, or soft protective material.
  • Send extra units (optional) — especially for breakables or reflective items.
  • Specify product details — ingredients, textures, packaging notes.
  • Provide usage instructions (optional) — for interactive or special products.
  • Exchange brand guidelines — include colors, backgrounds, or styling preferences.
  • Provide a mood board or visual references — to match your brand tone.
  • Include return instructions — with address or courier details if needed.

DON’Ts

  • Don't ship damaged, dirty, or used products — they reduce quality and can’t be fixed in post.
  • Don't ship last minute — always confirm shoot timelines first.
  • Don't assume product usage — always clarify with notes or a demo.
  • Don't skip proper packaging — fragile items must be protected.
  • Don't mix product categories — keep cosmetics, garments, etc., separated and labeled.
  • Don't omit key styling info — include angles, must-haves, disclaimers, etc.
  • Don't forget legal or label approvals — use final packaging only.
